Q3, 2021

 

 

Starting weight: 192(?) lbs

...Note: Same as q2: This is very heavy for me. Typically I am 172 - 176

Starting fitness level: Intermediate. I finished an 8 week training routine in q2. Gained strength and muscle mass.

...Note: Body is still a bit "creeky" after 1 year of limited activity due to covid quarantining, no traveling, no sports. I had been doing light home workouts 3x week. But nothing to prevent 20 lbs of weight gain.

Goals: 196 -- 193 -- 190 -- 192 --189 -- 186 -- 188 (end of September)

Enjoy summer but don't get back up to 192. Try to prioritize active travel. Always accept opportunities to walk far.

General attainable weight loss guidance: Lose 3 lbs per month, allow 3rd month to gain 2 back. So 196--193--190--192

Loose goal: 188. That would be 3 pounds per month.

This strategy allows more passive weight loss without major unsistainable lifestyle changes.

 

 

Most of Q3 spent traveling -- Goal is to do home workout 4 - 5 days / week.


Accept all invitations for active activities. Long walks, hikes, etc. Still eat and drink as you like. Let activity handle weight loss goals.
Example workouts: Pushups, incline push ups, planks, 1 legged squats, side dumbbell (backpack) delt raise, dumbbell (backpack) curls, back-focused push up, 1 legged glute raise
Remember to do 1 week stretching / yoga at the end of the quarter.
Note: I also had a red patch develop on my chest, and redness in my face.
This turned out to likely be fungal: I showered 2-3x/day and treated with econazole/mikonazole 2-3 times per day. After 6 weeks, the patches were reduced significantly.
Also changed towels every 2-3 days and washed clothes after every use. Will seek help from dermatologist. Previously treated as psoriasis with little results.
