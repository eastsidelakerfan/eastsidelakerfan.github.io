the Thriving Rhododendron



These photos are from the property that I grew up on.
I always appreciated that the space was beautiful.
In my adult life, I'd learn just how proud we should be (and how fortunate we are).
On this page are photos of a rhododendron bush -- one that is absolutely thriving.
A rhododendron of this size and magnificence is unique.
