Q2, 2021

 

 

Starting weight: 198 lbs

...Note: This is very heavy for me. Typically I am 172 - 176

Starting fitness level: Beginner

...Note: I am completely deconditioned, mostly just sitting in covid quarantine. Body is "creeky." I had been doing light home workouts 3x week. But nothing to prevent 20 lbs of weight gain.

Goals:

First 4 weeks: Habit form. Go to gym 3x/week and stick to some routine (written below).

Next 4 weeks: Hypertrophy training, high volume: Go to gym 5 - 6 days/w and focus on hypertrophy program.

Next 2 weeks: Lose some weight. Do light workouts 2x / day.
